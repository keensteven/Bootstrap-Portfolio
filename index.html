About

I'm Steven Keen – a graphic designer with over fifteen years of professional experience in the field. I hold a B.A. in Visual Arts and Graphic Design from the University of Maryland. I’ve had quite a few jobs in the design industry – from production artist to graphic designer – and I’ve learned a lot along the way. I’ve produced work for universities, trade associations, federal government agencies, international companies and small businesses. I thrive on new challenges and am eager to expand my experience while applying my creative skills. Please feel free to contact me with any comments or questions, I'm always interested in meeting new people.
